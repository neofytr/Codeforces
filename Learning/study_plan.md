## Roadmap (Newbie to Specialist)

**Newbie (800-1000)**
- Basic implementation, brute force, simple math, sorting

**Pupil (1000-1200)**
- Greedy, prefix sums, two pointers, basic binary search, simple DP

**Specialist (1200-1400)**
- Intermediate DP, graphs (BFS/DFS/Dijkstra), segment trees, DSU, number theory, combinatorics

Focus: solve rating-appropriate problems consistently, upsolve +200 above current rating, and participate in every round.
